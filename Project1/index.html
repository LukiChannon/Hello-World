Parrots are colorful, intelligent birds known for their ability to mimic sounds and human speech. They live mainly in tropical and subtropical regions and come in many species, from tiny parakeets to large macaws. Parrots are social, curious, and often form strong bonds with their owners, making them popular pets. Their bright feathers and playful personalities make them stand out in the animal world.

THE BEAR
Eagles are powerful birds of prey known for their sharp vision, strong talons, and impressive wingspans. Found across many parts of the world, they are skilled hunters that soar high above landscapes in search of food. Often seen as symbols of strength and freedom, eagles play an important role in nature by keeping ecosystems balanced.
